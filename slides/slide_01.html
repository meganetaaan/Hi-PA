Speech Recognition

How Speech Recognition Work

  • Model: Hidden Markov Model + Recurrent Neural Network

  1. Signal Analysis

  2. Language modelling  :
          probabilistic distribution of language

    1. Probability of the word itself

    2. Conditional probability of the word
      coming next to the previous words

How Speech Recognition Work

  • "Good programmers know what to _________."
  • frequency = P("write")
  • conditional = P("write"|"Good programmers know what to")
                                signal             frequency            conditional
  • wrought            0.3                     0.001                     0.001
  •    right                0.2                       0.1                         0.01
  •    write               0.1                      0.05                         0.2
